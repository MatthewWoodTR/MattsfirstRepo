Division enhancement: Option to hide Division numbers on reports

1. Customer Problem
Division numbers clutter financials and confuse external readers.

2. Customer Research
Roadmap gray bar; requested in Table 1.

3. Our Solution
- Report setting to hide division numbers while preserving names/labels.
- Defaults configurable at engagement or firm level.

4. Product Metrics
- Adoption: >60% of divisional reports use the setting at least once.
- Support tickets on report formatting: -30%.
