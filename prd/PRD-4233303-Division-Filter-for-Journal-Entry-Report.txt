[Product Roadmap Item Title]
Division enhancement: Division Filter for Journal Entry Report

1. Customer Problem
Reviewers need to see JEs by division quickly; current report lacks an easy division filter.

2. Customer Research (paraphrased)
Requested small, focused improvement with high review value. ADO notes classify it as short duration.

3. Our Solution
- Add division filter (single/multi‑select) to JE report UI and API.
- Persist last selection per engagement.

4. Product Metrics
- 50% reduction in time to produce a per‑division JE report.
- 75% usage among divisional engagements after 60 days.
