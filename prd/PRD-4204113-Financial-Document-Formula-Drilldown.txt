Financial Document Formula Drill-Down in Excel and Word

1. Customer Problem
Auditors need to trace numbers from financial statements back to underlying accounts quickly. Today they must run separate reports to troubleshoot.

2. Customer Research
Common request from multiple firms (see artifacts). Comp parity with PFX and CSA.

3. Our Solution
- In Word/Excel add-in, analyze a selected formula and show drill-down by account grouping/code and individual accounts with subtotals.
- Export drill results into a binder report.
Scope limits: No automatic cross-engagement linking changes.

4. Product Metrics
- Time to trace a number to accounts: -70%.
- Accuracy confidence (survey): +20 pts.
