Make note dialogs larger

1. Customer Problem
Current note dialogs are cramped, forcing excessive scrolling and making long reviews difficult.

2. Customer Research
Raised by Anchin; aligns with visibility enhancements initiative.

3. Our Solution
- Resize dialogs to support fullscreen or large modal sizes.
- Remember user preference per device.
- Ensure accessibility and responsive behavior.

4. Product Metrics
- Note creation/edit time: -20%.
- User satisfaction with note UX: +15 pts.
